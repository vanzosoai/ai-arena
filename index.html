1. THE GAME
AI Arena is a head-to-head weekly creativity challenge between John and Nick. Every week, both players must submit an AI-generated creation — an app, video, image, tool, anything built with AI. Whoever earns the most from their creation that week wins. If only one player submits, they claim the week by default. The goal: show up every week, build real things, and make money from them.
2. WEEKLY SUBMISSIONS
TITLE — Give your creation a name. Required.

DESCRIPTION — Describe what you built and how AI was used.

WEBSITE / PROJECT URL — Link to your live project. Must start with https:// for the screenshot preview to load correctly. A screenshot is auto-captured from the URL when you submit — no need to upload one separately unless the URL doesn't load a visual.

SOCIAL LINK — Optional TikTok, Instagram, or X link if you posted about it.

EARNINGS FROM AI THIS WEEK (USD) — Enter any money your AI creation generated this week. This is what determines the winner. Be honest — it's between you two.

PHOTO UPLOAD — If you don't have a URL, upload an image directly. Drag and drop or click to select. Max recommended size: 5MB.

Submissions lock in for the current week. You can re-submit to update before the week ends.
3. SCORING & EARNINGS
Each week is won by whoever earned more from their AI creation. The scoreboard tracks:

WEEKLY SCORE — Wins tracked across all weeks (shown next to each player's name).
EARNINGS — Shown per submission in the battle cards and history.
COMBINED STATS — The center panel shows total weeks, apps built, weeks where both showed up, and total combined earnings. These update live.
4. AVATAR STATES & PERKS
Your avatar changes based on your status each week:

🔥 LOCKED IN — You've submitted for the current week. Your avatar glows gold (John) or blue (Nick).
💀 GET BUSY — You didn't submit last week and haven't submitted yet this week. Your avatar goes greyscale, shakes, and a skull appears on their chest. A warning.
👑 LAST CHAMP — You won last week. A crown floats above your avatar's head and they glow orange until someone else wins.

⚔️ FIRST BLOOD — If you're the first to submit in a given week, you get the First Blood flash on screen and the other player gets a special email taunt.
5. EMAIL NOTIFICATIONS
When you submit your creation, the other player automatically gets an email letting them know. The email includes:

· Your creation title and description
· A randomized trash-talk taunt
· A link directly back to the arena
· A different message if both players have already submitted that week

Emails go to the address in your Account Settings. Update your email there to change where notifications are sent.
6. SPECTATOR MODE
Anyone can watch without an account. On the login screen, click 👁 SPECTATE to enter the arena in read-only mode. You'll see all current and past submissions, scores, and stats — but cannot submit. Music plays for spectators too. The settings gear is hidden in spectator mode.
7. HALL OF CARNAGE
Every completed week lives in the Hall of Carnage — the battle history at the bottom of the arena. Each week shows both submissions, the winner, and earnings. Weeks with only one submission show who showed up and who didn't. Expand any week to see the full cards, or click any card to open a full detail view. Use the search bar to find any past creation by title or description. This is the permanent record.
8. ACCOUNT SETTINGS
Access via the ⚙️ gear button in the header (players only — hidden for spectators).

CHANGE EMAIL — Updates your login email and your notification address for weekly emails in one step.
CHANGE PASSWORD — Enter a new password (min 6 characters) and confirm it.
FORGOT PASSWORD — On the login screen, enter your email and a reset link will be sent. Click the link, then set a new password when the recovery screen appears.

Both players log in with email + password. There are no shared accounts — each player has their own credentials.
